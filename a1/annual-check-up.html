What’s Included in Your Annual Dental Check-Up

Physical Exam

Brief head, neck, gum and tooth assessment to spot early signs of problems.

Professional Clean

Gentle scale and polish to remove plaque and help prevent decay and gum disease.

X-Rays

Targeted images only when helpful for diagnosis and treatment planning.

Dental Check

Thorough cavity and gum check, plus a bite and wear review.

Nutritional Guidance

Simple, practical tips tailored to your family’s everyday routines.

Next-Step Plan

Clear advice if follow-up or optional tests are recommended.

Why Every Year?

  • Catch issues early—treatments are simpler and more affordable.
  • Preventive care keeps smiles bright and confident.
  • Great for kids, teens, adults and retirees in the Razole community.

How to Prepare

  • Arrive a few minutes early for forms if it’s your first visit.
  • Bring a current medication list and any questions.
  • Brush and floss as normal—no special prep needed.
