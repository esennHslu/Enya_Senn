What Happens During Your Free Check-Up

Your visit is designed to give you a clear picture of your current oral health and what you may need next.

Clinical Examination

One of our dentists will carefully examine your teeth, gums and mouth, checking for signs of decay, gum problems or other concerns that may need attention.

Professional Clean

You’ll receive a thorough clean to help remove plaque and tartar build-up, leaving your mouth feeling fresher and supporting healthier gums.

Tailored Advice

We’ll share practical tips specific to you, such as daily care routines and lifestyle suggestions, to help you maintain good oral health and reduce the risk of future issues.

Next Steps & Treatment Plan

If we find anything that needs follow-up, your dentist will outline a clear treatment plan and talk you through the options, so you can decide what works best for you.

Questions Welcome

This is also a good chance to ask about topics like sensitivity, cosmetic options or children’s dental care. Our team is happy to explain things in everyday language.

*Free check-up appointments are available on a first-come, first-served basis for the event date and time listed above.

Who Is This For?

  • Adults who haven’t seen a dentist in a while.
  • Families wanting a quick review of their dental health.
  • Anyone curious about their current oral health status.

Before You Come

  • Arrive a little early if it’s your first visit so we can sort any basic details.
  • Bring a list of medications and any dental concerns you’d like to discuss.
  • Brush as usual—no special preparation is required.
