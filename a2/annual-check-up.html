What’s Included in Your Annual Dental Check-Up*

Our Annual Dental Check-Up package is designed as a complete review of your oral health, with a focus on prevention and early detection.

Physical Examination

A thorough check of your mouth, jaw and surrounding areas to look for signs of illness, changes or discomfort that may need attention.

Professional Clean

Careful removal of plaque and tartar, leaving teeth feeling smoother and helping to protect against decay and gum disease.

X-Rays (If Indicated)

Targeted X-rays when helpful to assess teeth and bone, identify hidden problems and support preventive care planning.

Dental Check-Up

Detailed review of your teeth and gums, with advice on managing or reducing any signs of dental disease.

Nutritional Guidance

Conversation about your diet and daily habits, with simple suggestions that support a stronger, healthier smile.

Additional Tests (If Needed)

Where appropriate for your age, history or specific concerns, we may recommend extra tests or lab work to support a clear diagnosis.

*Your dentist will tailor the visit to your needs. Not all services are required for every patient.

Why Every Year?

  • Identify potential problems early, when treatment is usually simpler and more affordable.
  • Support long-term oral and general health through regular preventive care.
  • Suitable for children, teens, adults and retirees in the Razole community.

How to Prepare

  • Arrive a little early if it’s your first visit, so we can complete any forms together.
  • Bring a list of medications and any questions or concerns you’d like to discuss.
  • Brush and floss as normal—no special preparation is needed.
