About 100SMILES Dental Care

100SMILES Dental Care is a modern clinic located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole. We have been part of the local community for more than thirty years, and our roots here run deep. Across our team, we share over fifty years of experience in dental care, and we are committed to delivering the best possible treatment for every patient.

We offer a wide range of services: from routine wellness check-ups and cleans to implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry and emergency care. Our dentists are equipped to manage common dental problems as well as more complex cases, providing diagnosis, treatment and surgery when needed.

Patient education is an important part of what we do. We help you understand your options and give you clear, practical information so you can make confident decisions about your dental health. Whether you need nutritional guidance, oral hygiene tips, root canal treatment or urgent support, our goal is to support both your health and your happiness.

With the addition of a convenient online booking option and our growing team—now including a dedicated pediatric dentist—100SMILES Dental Care continues to focus on accessible, high-quality care for every stage of life.
