Our New Dental Technologies

To deliver the best possible care, 100SMILES Dental Care has recently integrated several modern technologies into our practice.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution digital X-rays with significantly reduced radiation, helping us diagnose issues more quickly and accurately.

Intraoral Cameras

Small cameras that capture detailed images inside your mouth, so we can detect problems early and show you what we see.

Laser Dentistry

Gentle laser techniques for selected treatments, supporting minimally invasive care with less discomfort and faster healing.

CAD/CAM Restorations

Computer-aided design and manufacturing that allows us to create precise crowns, bridges and veneers, often in a single visit.

3D Printing

3D-printed models, guides and custom appliances that improve treatment planning and enhance the fit of your dental devices.

Teledentistry

Virtual consultations for suitable dental concerns, allowing you to receive advice and follow-up care from the comfort of home.
