Welcome to 100SMILES Dental Care

For more than three decades, 100SMILES Dental Care has been part of the Razole community at 33 Fantail Street. With over fifty years of combined experience, our team offers friendly, reliable and compassionate dental care for every age.

We look after everyday dental needs and more complex care in one place—from routine check-ups and professional cleans to implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry and emergency support. Our clinicians focus on clear explanations, gentle treatment and practical tips you can use at home.

To make life easier, we’ve introduced an online booking option alongside phone and email appointments, and we’ve welcomed a dedicated pediatric dentist so families can access care for children and adults under one roof.

Whether you’re due for your Annual Dental Check-Up, considering implants or simply want better day-to-day oral care routines, we’re here to help keep your smile healthy and confident.
